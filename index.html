03 · The operating model

The Fragility–Autonomy Matrix

Every agent earns autonomy through clean approvals — Report, then Suggest, then Do — and every task carries a fragility class: the cost of getting it wrong. The two together make the nine-box model the whole company runs on.

Autonomy
Low fragility
Medium fragility
High fragility

Drafts the action. One tap to approve.
